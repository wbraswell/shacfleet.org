Minto Rendezvous 2013

Flotsam Flotilla 2013 - See photos here: https://plus.google.com/photos/106496702885477167595/albums/5922188547947543009

The Commodore's Challenge:
1. The basic components of your vessel are limited to sheet plastic and wood.
2. Sheet plastic will not be defined.
3. You may use hardware if necessary.
4. The vessel may not weigh over 25 pounds.

Special awards will be given for:
1st Place
Creativity
Innovation of Design
Humor
