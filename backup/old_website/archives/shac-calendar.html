Events
  • January 19-21 - Sea Scout Academy (Regional Event hosted by SHAC)  Camp Mohawk in Alvin: See attached files for Boarding Manual and registration form.
  • January 26 - Scouting for Food
  • February 2 - Scouting for Food
  • February 9 - University of Scouting
  • March 15-17 - Venturing Rendezvous: Grab your tent and a few good friends for a fun-filled weekend in the woods at Camp Strake.
  • April 13 - Scout Fair: Develop an activity, teach a skill, and show the Cub Scouts what they can be when they grow up.
  • April 27 - Sail Daze: Houston Yacht Club, 9:00-3:00: Bring your small boats and lots of friends for a day of fun on Galveston Bay.
  • June 9-15 - NYLT (coed)
  • June 23-29 - Venturing Rough Rider Week, El Rancho Cima
  • July 14-20 - Venturing Road to Ranger
  • August 9-11- Venturing Summit at Camp Strake: Venturers and Sea Scouts will gather for a weekend of fun, fellowship, and an opportunity to share "A Last Aloha" with Camp Strake. More information is available at www.shacventuring.org
  • September 6-8 - Minto Rendezvous (Regional Event hosted by SHAC), Bovay Scout Ranch, Navasota: Start practicing now to showcase your nautical skills at our traditional rendezvous.  See attached files for Boarding Manual, guidelines for the 2013 Flotsam Flotilla and registration form. Link to Minto Photos: https://plus.google.com/photos/106496702885477167595/albums/5922188547947543009
  • September 28 - The Aggie Cup: 2013 Koch Cup Southern Region Qualifying Regatta - Photos: https://plus.google.com/photos/106496702885477167595/albums/5935872405565229169
  • October 12-14: USPS Sea School, Lakewood Yacht Club. See application below. Link to Sea School photos: https://plus.google.com/photos/106496702885477167595/albums/5935879933890206689
  • October 26 - Fall Regatta: Deussen Park, Lake Houston, 9:00-4:00:  Bring out the small boats for racing in orthodox and unorthodox ways for bragging rights and the "Commodore's Bowl."
  • November 2 - Council Bridge of Honor (location to be determined): SHAC Fleet will recognize youth and ships for their advancement, honors and awards.
  • November 9-10 - Fleet Adult Sail
  • November 15-17 - Seabadge (National training hosted by SHAC): Announcement and registration are attached. Link to Sebadge photos: https://plus.google.com/photos/106496702885477167595/albums/5948397656714743137
  • December 26-31 - NYLT (coed)
